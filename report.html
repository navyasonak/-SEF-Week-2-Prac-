About this report

The Office of the Australian Information Commissioner(OAIC) peroidically publishes information about notification recieved under the Notification Data Breaches(NDB)scheme to assist entities and the public to understand the operation of the scheme. This report captures notifications made under the NDB scheme for the period from 1 July to 31 December 2020.

NDB scheme statistics in this report are current as of 8 January 2021. However, a number of notifications included in these statistics are still under assessment and their status and categorisation are subject to change.
